Privacy Policy for Hourly Chime

Last updated: May 20, 2026

Data Collection

Hourly Chime does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All app settings and preferences are stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults and are never sent to any server.

Third-Party Services

Hourly Chime does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. The app makes no network requests.

Notifications

The app requests notification permissions solely to deliver hourly chime alerts at your scheduled times. Notification preferences are managed entirely through iOS Settings.

Background Audio

The app uses background audio processing to ensure timely delivery of chime notifications. No audio data is recorded or transmitted.

Children

Hourly Chime does not knowingly collect data from children. The app is rated 4+ and contains no objectionable content.

Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
