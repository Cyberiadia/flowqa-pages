Common Questions

Q: Why aren't my chimes playing?
A: Make sure notifications are enabled in iOS Settings > Hourly Chime. Also check that your schedule start/end times and the chime toggle in the app are set correctly.

Q: Can I change the chime sound?
A: Yes! Open the app, tap the gear icon, and choose from 10 different sounds in the Sound section.

Q: Does the app work in the background?
A: Yes, Hourly Chime uses background audio to deliver timely chime notifications even when the app isn't open.
